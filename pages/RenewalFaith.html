Vision & Mission
  • Vision: Inspired by St. Vincent Pallotti, our founder and urged by the love of Christ we shall build up The Union of the Catholic Apostolate.
  • Missions:
    1. To revive and deepen our Charism among the members.
    2. To help every Indian to understand his/her own faith as means to promote human harmony and build a just society in our motherland.
    3. To prepare suitable training programmes towards this network with people of similar mind and become a national and international repository in formation and action programmes.
