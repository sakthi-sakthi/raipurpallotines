MISSION SECRETARIAT

The mission secretariat of the province is an auxiliary body of the Provincial administration. Its main function is to coordinate and assist the work of the Province in mission territories. It also works towards collaboration of laity and their active role in the proclamation of the Gospel. It works in collaboration with the General Secretariat for the Missions.

Objectives:

  • To generate funds locally
  • To look for sponsors from our institutions / Parishes etc.
  • To write projects for mission, school buildings, boarding, vehicles, sound systems etc.
