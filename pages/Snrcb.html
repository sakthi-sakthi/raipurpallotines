Vision

· Inspired by St. Vincent Pallotti, our founder and urged by the love of Christ we shall build up The Union of the Catholic Apostolate.
· In order to welcome the faithful – Laity, Religious and Clergy to support and challenge one another to live their faith and vocation responsibly and enable every member of Union of the Catholic Apostolate to undertake every possible task to make the Church witnessing and evangelising.
· In order to coordinate and collaborate with all people of good will to create a harmonious and just society in our country.

Mission

· To revive and deepen our Charism among the members.
· To help every Indian to understand his/her own faith as means to promote human harmony and build a just society in our motherland.
· To prepare suitable training programmes towards this network with people of similar mind and become a national and international repository in formation and action programmes.

Retreats and Programmes

  • Retreats for Priests and religious (Short and long term)
  • Retreats for Laity, Children, Youth, couples and Family
  • Counselling facility for all
  • Leadership training/ Empowerment Programmes for Laity
  • Bible Course
  • Study and research centre in Spirituality and Charism
  • Priests are available for the sacrament of Reconciliation and counselling
  • Holy mass intentions are accepted
  • Retreats, Seminars and chapters may be organised for the groups on request
